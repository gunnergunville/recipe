Classic KD

Boil 6-cups water. Cook 6 to 8 minutes.
until tender, stirring occasionally.

Drain; Return to saucepan.

Add 1 Tbsp. non hydrogenated margarine, 1/3 cup
skim milk and the cheese sauce mix

Mix well
